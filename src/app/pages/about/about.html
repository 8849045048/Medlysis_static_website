About Medlysis

We are a London-registered healthcare solutions provider with dedicated operations in India, delivering fast, secure, and timely support to U.S. healthcare organizations.

Our Story

Medlysis started with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between the complex demands of U.S. healthcare and the need for efficient, scalable support. We saw the challenges healthcare providers, legal firms, and insurance companies faced in accessing timely and compliant information, so we created a purpose-built solution.

Our UK entity provides a robust, London-based legal and adherence to stringent regulatory frameworks, while our skilled operations team in India ensures high- quality, 24/7 service. This "Global Footprint" gives our clients the best of both worlds: robust governance and operational agility.

Our Mission

To deliver accurate, secure, and timely support services to U.S. healthcare partners, helping them reduce administrative burdens, ensure compliance, and achieve their strategic goals through reliable partnership and innovative solutions.

Commitment to Compliance

Security is our foundation. We are ICO registered and adhere to the highest standards to protect sensitive healthcare information.
